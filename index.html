Our Mission

Music for every child, everywhere

Soundly is an organization dedicated to helping children around the world discover and learn music — especially those who may not otherwise have access, including visually impaired students. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to experience music from a young age, no matter their circumstances.

Soundly was founded by a student who has been learning piano for the past 9 years and is passionate about sharing that knowledge with others.

We partner with schools across the globe to bring music education to their students. However, many of these schools lack even the most basic resources — some don't have access to a single piano.

That's why we're launching this GoFundMe: to raise money to purchase and donate pianos and other equipment to schools in need, opening the door for countless children to begin their musical journey.

With your support, we can make music accessible to every child — one piano at a time.
