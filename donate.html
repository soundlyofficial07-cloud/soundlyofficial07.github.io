Why we need your help

Soundly partners with schools across the globe to bring music education to students who need it most. But when we arrive, we often find the same heartbreaking reality: classrooms full of children who want to learn, and no piano in sight.

A single donated piano can serve an entire school for decades. It becomes the center of recitals, lessons, and moments of joy that children carry with them for the rest of their lives.

Every dollar raised through this campaign goes directly toward purchasing and shipping instruments to schools in need — along with sheet music, braille stickers, and other learning materials.
